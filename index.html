The lessons I learned on the field—teamwork, accountability, communication, and perseverance carried over into the classroom and my cybersecurity studies. I’ve learned how to handle pressure, adapt to changing situations, and stay committed even when schedules are tight or challenges arise. The ability to push through tough practices and long nights of studying has prepared me for the demands of a cybersecurity career. I believe that my background in athletics has strengthened not only my technical skills, but also my mindset giving me the tools to thrive in collaborative, high-stakes environments.
